A Stepping Stone for Greatness.

It’s not about us but about what we can do for you! Our mission is to develop you as as a player to reach your ultimate goal of becoming a Pro, a College player, or whatever you want to be. Our emphasis is on being that environment that makes it easy to improve and takes care of all the fine details so you can focus on what’s important -- Learning & Loving the Game.

Our Humble Beginnings

“I created Trilogy Soccer with the sole focus on giving back to a community of hungry youth soccer players that desire guidance in order to become great. As a player myself, soccer opened so many opportunities in my life that I felt the need to pave the way for others to follow and become successful. I made it my mission to help as many players as possible reach the next level and prepare them for all of the obstacles that stand in their way. It’s not about me or my staff, but about our vision to make developing as easy as possible. That way how far each player goes is up to them!”

Coach Francisco J. Perez

Founder of Trilogy Soccer Former D1 Soccer Athlete UC Berkeley

Our Mission Statement

Our mission at Trilogy Soccer is to help soccer players reach their full potential. This is only achieved by doing more than everyone else & pushing yourself consistently. Therefore we created an environment that makes it easy to improve yourself ALL YEAR LONG.
