Our Philosophy & Training Methods

At Trilogy Soccer, we pride ourselves on providing quality soccer training that is organized, challenging, and methodical. Our main focus is on developing sound individuals because, although soccer is a team sport, decisions and actions are made by individuals. We've meticulously crafted our programs to prioritize individual player development above all else. By limiting group sizes, we ensure that each player receives personalized attention, allowing our coaches to tailor instruction to meet the unique needs of every participant. Our 60-minute sessions are designed to maximize training intensity and focus, promoting deep engagement and rapid skill acquisition. We believe in providing the right balance of challenge and support across technical, tactical, physical, and psychological aspects of the game, ensuring steady progression and tangible results for every player who walks through our doors. With Trilogy, players can expect a training experience that is truly transformative, empowering them to reach new heights in their soccer journey.

Our Training Programs

Skill Development Groups

Our Skill Development Groups offer a dynamic environment where players can enhance their individual technical and tactical skills alongside peers of similar age and level. With group sizes limited to 3-8 players, each participant receives personalized attention, fostering a deep focus on skill development. This small group setting not only facilitates better tactical understanding but also allows players to build up their technical foundations and then apply their skills in game-like situations, resulting in accelerated improvement and a competitive edge on the field.

  • 60 Minute Sessions
  • Small & Select Groups
  • Limit 3 - 8 players per group
  • Placed accordingly into Prodigy or Elite levels

Private Mentorship Program

Our Private Mentorship Program offers personalized coaching tailored to the unique needs and goals of each player, whether they prefer one-on-one attention or semi-private sessions. With a commitment of at least 4 sessions, participants receive focused guidance and support to elevate their game to the next level. Limited availability ensures a dedicated focus on each player's development, maximizing their potential on the field.

  • 60 Minute Sessions
  • 1:1 Private or Semi Private Small Groups
  • Limited availability by coach
  • Must be approved before purchasing bundles & scheduling sessions
