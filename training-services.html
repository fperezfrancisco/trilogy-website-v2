Our Philosophy & Training Methods

Here at Trilogy Soccer, we pride ourselves in providing quality soccer training that is organized, challenging, and methodical. Our main focus is on developing sound individuals, because although soccer is thought of as a team sport, decisions and actions are made by individuals.

Each training focuses & incorporates the 4 components important to each individual soccer athlete:

Technical Component Tactical Component Physical Component Psycholigical Component

In order to keep our athletes well balanced, we make sure each player has the technical & physical foundational tools to compete while also demonstrating & explaining HOW to use & apply them in game-like situations.

From basic, fundamental aspects to high-level, collegiate/pro details, we cover it all and ensure you are prepared to perform like a pro.

Open Group Training

Training that is open to all of our Trilogy athletes who are between the ages of 6-18 playing competitive soccer. We ensure to develop skill with a rigorous technical, tactical, and physical curriculum focused on improving the individual athlete at each moment. Each session is 60 minutes and offered at least once a week (usually twice). Each group is limited to maximum number of players in order to keep the groups small in size for more focus and repetitions. Use our booking app to reserve your spot at the next group session available!

Elite Group Training

Our brand new program that brings the extra exclusivity, details, and intensity to keep up with your individual needs. All players must be invited or apply online and be accepted in order to sign up for our Elite Group Training. Each session is 90 minutes long focusing on technical, tactical, and physical components of the game in depth. Fill out the application to get started and take a look at our availability if you are already an Elite Athlete. All sessions are capped at maximum 8 players per session.

Private Training

Training in a 1-on-1 basis. We make sure each session is curated specifically to your individual needs technically, tactically, physically, and psychologically. Each session is 60 minutes and must be booked at least one day in advanced. We require at lest 4 hours notice for cancellations. We highly encourage all players to contact us for guidance in booking individual sessions especially if new to our training programs.
