Introducing our

Prodigy Program

Introducing our Prodigy Program: a 6-week training for ages 6-9 to develop fundamental soccer skills and become more competitive. Focus areas include dribbling, passing, receiving, and finishing, along with balance and agility. Join us to elevate your child's game!

Program Overview

Why choose our prodigy program?

Our Prodigy Program is designed to transform recreational and youth soccer players into more competitive athletes. Over six weeks, your child will focus on essential skills like dribbling, passing, receiving, and finishing, as well as soccer-specific physical motor skills like balance and agility. With quality coaches, top-notch organization, and a player-centric training environment, we quickly prepare players for their soccer journey ahead.

Program Benefits

  • Consistent training program and environment that allows youth players to progressively learn and develop
  • Mastery of individual technical soccer skills - passing, receiving, & dribbling
  • Mastery of individual physical abilities - speed, agility, balance, and quickness
  • Professional soccer coaches & trainers with multiple years of experience at the club, academy, and collegiate level
  • Extra attention and a proper training plan that helps players advance quicker than only training on a recreational team or club
  • An environment that is professional, organized, and challenging to the athlete
  • A safe and engaging environment that puts players first
