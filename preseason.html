Preseason Program

Prepare with a purpose this summer & stay ahead of the competition with our newest program that trains you like a D1 college athlete.

Introduction

Welcome to the Trilogy Preseason Prep Program, designed exclusively for serious soccer players aged 10 and up. Our intensive five-day-a-week program focuses on technical skill development, tactical application, mobility, resistance training, endurance, and game scenarios to ensure players are primed for success in their upcoming seasons.

  • June 10th - June 14th
  • June 17th - June 21st
  • June 24th - June 28th
  • July 8th - July 12th

Program Overview

  • Objective: Prepare serious soccer players aged 10 and up for success in their upcoming seasons.
  • Duration: Four-week program running from June 10th to July 12th.
  • All sessions are roughly 80 minutes
  • Monday & Wednesday: Technical sharpness and tactical application in game scenarios.
  • Tuesday & Thursday: Mobility, resistance training, endurance training, and small-sided games.
  • Friday: Endurance test followed by fun games (session duration may be one hour).
  • Session Capacity: Limited players per session to ensure personalized attention and coaching. (based on many factors including coach-to-player ratio, availability, & skill level)

Benefits

  • Structured curriculum designed for progressive development.
  • Intensive training regimen to enhance technical skills, tactical understanding, mobility, and endurance.
  • Opportunities for small-sided games to apply learned skills in a game setting.
  • Weekly endurance tests to track progress and enhance fitness levels.
  • Exposure to a training program/schedule similar to what players can expect with a professional academy or Division 1 college program.
